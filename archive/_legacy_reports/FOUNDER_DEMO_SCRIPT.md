# NeuronX Founder Demo Script (7 Minutes)

## Pre-Demo Setup
- **Environment**: NeuronX Demo Tenant (GHL sub-account with Gold snapshot installed).
- **URL**: Have the landing page URL ready.
- **Browser**: Two tabs: (1) Public landing page, (2) GHL Dashboard (Opportunities view).

---

## MINUTE 1: Opening Hook (Problem Framing)

**Say**:
> "Let me show you why immigration firms lose 60% of their leads before they ever book a consultation."

**Do**:
- Open a blank Google Sheet or whiteboard screen.
- Draw a funnel: "100 Inquiries → 40 Contacted → 20 Booked → 8 Retained".

**Say**:
> "The problem isn't your service. It's the chaos between 'inquiry' and 'consultation'. Leads wait hours for a response. Follow-ups fall through the cracks. Consultants walk into meetings blind. That's $50K+ in lost revenue every single month."

**Transition**:
> "NeuronX fixes this. Let me show you the system in 6 minutes."

---

## MINUTE 2: Lead Submission (Public Experience)

**Say**:
> "This is what your prospects see. A professional, credible landing page."

**Do**:
- Switch to Tab 1: Landing page.
- Scroll from Hero → Trust Signals → Programs → Form.

**Say**:
> "No clutter. No hype. Just clear, compliant messaging. This page is built into the snapshot. It's yours the day we install."

**Fill the Form LIVE**:
- First Name: "Demo"
- Last Name: "Prospect"
- Email: `demo@example.com`
- Phone: `+16135551234`
- Program: "Express Entry"
- Location: "India"
- Timeline: "3-6 months"

**Click Submit**.

**Say**:
> "The moment I click submit, the system wakes up."

---

## MINUTE 3: Backend Automation (Pipeline Entry)

**Do**:
- Switch to Tab 2: GHL Dashboard (Opportunities > Pipeline).
- Refresh the page.

**Say**:
> "There. 'Demo Prospect' just entered the pipeline. NEW stage. In under 10 seconds."

**Click the contact card**.

**Say**:
> "Look at the conversation history. The system already sent an acknowledgment SMS and email. Instantly. While you were still on your coffee break."

**Read the SMS aloud** (paraphrased):
> "Hi Demo, thank you for contacting NeuronX Immigration Advisory. Our team will be in touch shortly."

**Say**:
> "Professional. Compliant. Fast. That's the first impression that separates you from firms who respond 4 hours later."

**Transition**:
> "But that's just the start. Let me show you the automation."

---

## MINUTE 4: Workflow Automation (The Brain)

**Do**:
- Navigate to: Automation → Workflows.
- Show the list (WF-01 to WF-11).

**Say**:
> "These 11 workflows are the operating system. They handle everything from 'new inquiry' to 'retainer signed'."

**Click WF-01 (New Inquiry Acknowledge)**.

**Say**:
> "This one fires when the form is submitted. It creates the contact, assigns tags, moves them into the pipeline, sends the SMS and email, and queues the next step—all in one second."

**Click WF-02 (Contact Attempt Sequence)**.

**Say**:
> "This one handles follow-up. If your team doesn't reach them in 30 minutes, it sends a booking link via SMS. If still no contact, it tries again in 2 hours. Then the next day. Then 2 days. Then 5 days. Then 10 days. It never gives up. That's 7 touch points across 2 weeks. Automatically."

**Transition**:
> "And when they book? The system handles that too."

---

## MINUTE 5: Booking & Consultation Flow

**Do**:
- Navigate to: Calendars → "Immigration Consultations".
- Show the calendar view.

**Say**:
> "When a lead books, they get immediate confirmation. Then reminders: 48 hours before. 24 hours. 2 hours. All automatic."

**Do**:
- Navigate back to Opportunities.
- Click a contact in "BOOKED" stage (if sample data exists, otherwise describe).

**Say**:
> "The moment they book, they move to 'BOOKED'. Your consultant sees them in the queue. And here's the magic: they get a consultation prep briefing with all the lead's context. Program interest. Timeline. Location. Every conversation. So they walk in prepared."

**Transition**:
> "And after the consultation?"

---

## MINUTE 6: Post-Consultation & Conversion

**Say**:
> "The consultant records the outcome. 'Proceed', 'Follow-up', or 'Declined'. The system takes it from there."

**Do**:
- Show WF-09 (Retainer Follow-Up) in the Workflows list.

**Say**:
> "If they say 'yes', the system sends the retainer agreement within the hour. Then it follows up. Day 1: check-in SMS. Day 3: email. Day 10: consultant call. Day 14: final notice. If they don't sign, they move to nurture. If they do, they move to 'RETAINED'."

**Do**:
- Show the Pipeline view with all 9 stages.

**Say**:
> "This is what visibility looks like. You see every lead, every stage, in real time. No spreadsheets. No guessing. You know exactly where your pipeline is leaking."

---

## MINUTE 7: Close & ROI

**Say**:
> "So here's what you get: instant response, structured follow-up, automated booking, prepared consultations, persistent conversion sequences, and complete visibility. All built on GoHighLevel. All delivered as a snapshot. We install it in 24 hours."

**Pause**.

**Say**:
> "Your average retainer is $2,500. If this system helps you close just one extra client per month, it pays for itself. Most firms see 3 to 5 additional retainers in the first 90 days."

**Ask**:
> "Which part of your pipeline is bleeding the most right now: response speed, follow-up, or consultant prep?"

**Listen**.

**Close**:
> "Let's get you set up. I'll send the onboarding link. You'll be live by tomorrow."

---

## Post-Demo Notes
1. **Record this demo as a Loom** for async sharing.

3. **Personalize the close** based on their answer to the "bleeding" question.
