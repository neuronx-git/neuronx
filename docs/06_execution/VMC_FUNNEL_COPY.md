## FAQ SECTION

**Section Title**: Frequently Asked Questions

### Q: What is an RCIC?
A Regulated Canadian Immigration Consultant (RCIC) is a licensed professional authorized by the College of Immigration and Citizenship Consultants (CICC) to represent clients in immigration matters. All VMC consultants hold active RCIC licenses.

### Q: How much does a consultation cost?
We offer a free initial assessment to understand your situation. Detailed consultations and service fees are discussed transparently before any engagement. No hidden costs.

### Q: Can you help with a refused application?
Yes. Refusal recovery is one of our specialties. We review the refusal reasons, identify presentation gaps, and resubmit a stronger application. We've successfully overturned hundreds of refusals.

### Q: How long does the immigration process take?
Timelines vary by program. Express Entry can take 6-12 months. Work permits 2-8 weeks. Study permits 4-16 weeks. During your consultation, we'll provide a realistic timeline for your specific situation.

### Q: Do I need to be in Canada to use your services?
No. We serve clients worldwide. All consultations can be conducted virtually. Whether you're in Canada or abroad, we can help.

### Q: What programs do you specialize in?
Express Entry, Provincial Nominee Programs, Study Permits, Work Permits, Family Sponsorship, Visitor/Super Visas, LMIA applications, and Refusal Recovery. We handle immigration end-to-end.
