# NeuronX Landing Page UX Improvement Checklist

## 1. Trust Signals
- [ ] **Regulatory Badges**: Add CICC (College of Immigration and Citizenship Consultants) logo in the hero section and footer.
- [ ] **License Disclosure**: Add "Consultations provided by [Name], RCIC #[Number]" in the footer.
- [ ] **Success Metrics**: Add a section "Why Choose Us" with stats like "10+ Years Experience", "Thousands of Cases Managed".
- [ ] **Security Badge**: Add "SSL Secure" or "Data Encrypted" icon near the form submit button.

## 2. Social Proof
- [ ] **Testimonials**: Add a "Client Success Stories" section with 3 short quotes.
- [ ] **Google Reviews**: Embed a widget or static image of 5-star rating summary.

## 3. Clarity & Expectation Setting
- [ ] **Process Steps**: Add a "How It Works" section:
    1.  Submit Inquiry
    2.  Initial Assessment (AI & Human Review)
    3.  Consultation with Expert
    4.  Retainer & Action
- [ ] **Consultant Bio**: Add a "Meet Your Advisor" section on the booking page (or landing page if single page).
- [ ] **Pricing Transparency**: Mention "Consultation Fee: $[Amount]" clearly on the booking page to reduce sticker shock/drop-off.

## 4. Compliance Text
- [ ] **Disclaimer**: Add "We are a private consultancy, not the government of Canada."
- [ ] **No Guarantee**: Add "Success is not guaranteed; assessment is based on current regulations."
- [ ] **Privacy Policy**: Link to Privacy Policy in the footer.

## 5. Design & Polish
- [ ] **Custom CSS**: Style the GHL form to match the premium brand (remove default shadows, better fonts).
- [ ] **Hero Image**: Use a high-quality, relevant image (e.g., Canadian landscape or professional office) instead of generic stock.
- [ ] **Mobile Responsiveness**: Verify the form and calendar widget display correctly on mobile devices.
