## Executive Summary

NeuronX occupies an **uncontested market position**: AI-powered sales and intake automation for immigration consulting firms. Every competitor (CaseEasy, Officio, Docketwise, Visto.ai) focuses on case management AFTER the client signs. Nobody automates the inquiry-to-retainer pipeline BEFORE signing. This is NeuronX's unfair advantage.

**Target**: 50 firms × $1,000/mo avg = $600K ARR in 18 months
**Method**: Founder-led sales → referral engine → content + community → scale

## 2. Sales Model: Founder-Led → Referral → Scale

### 2.1 Why Founder-Led Sales (Evidence)

- Jason Lemkin (SaaStr): "If the founder can't sell it, a sales rep definitely can't"
- First Round Capital: "Hiring salespeople before 20 closed deals is the #1 cause of death in B2B startups"
- Paul Graham (YC): "Take extraordinary measures to acquire and delight your first users"
- Clio, Jobber, ServiceTitan, Jane App — ALL started with founder-led sales
- At $500-1,500/mo ACV, OpenView data shows Sales-Assisted model outperforms pure PLG

### 2.2 Why NOT These Approaches

| Approach | Why NOT for NeuronX |
|----------|-------------------|
| Cold calling | 1-2% connect rate. RCICs screen calls. Worst B2B channel |
| Self-serve signup | At $500+/mo, buyers need guided demos. PLG fails above $200 ACV |
| Hire sales team first | Premature before 20 closed deals. Burns cash without proven playbook |
| Google/LinkedIn Ads | Wasteful before proving demo-to-close rate with manual outreach |
| Free trial | 2-5% conversion on opt-in trials for complex B2B. Use paid pilots instead |

### 3.2 The Founder Sales Sequence

```
Step 1: Build "100 Firms" list
  → CICC register → Ontario → 2+ consultants → has website with form
  → Prioritize 0-4 year firms (54% of market)

Step 2: LinkedIn warm outreach
  → Personalized connection note (mention their firm)
  → Share value post first (lead response time stats)
  → Offer: "Built something that cuts response to 5 min — 20-min demo?"
  → 30-40% accept rate, 2-5% convert to demo

Step 3: 30-min live demo
  → Show THEIR website form → NeuronX processes → AI calls in 5 min
  → 75% of B2B buyers say demo is most influential interaction (Gartner)
  → Clear CTA: "Ready to start?"

Step 4: 7-day close sequence
  → Day 0: Recap + case study
  → Day 2: "Any questions?"
  → Day 5: "2 onboarding slots left this month"
  → Day 7: Final follow-up
  → Target: 20-25% demo-to-close

Step 5: White-glove onboarding
  → Personally set up their sub-account
  → Walk through first real lead
  → Creates case study for next 10
```

## 12. The NeuronX GTM Guarantee Framework

### 12.1 Why This Strategy Has High Confidence

No strategy guarantees 100% success. But this one stacks **7 proven de-risking factors**:

| Factor | Evidence | Confidence |
|--------|----------|------------|
| **1. Uncontested positioning** | Zero competitors automate inquiry-to-retainer for immigration | High — blue ocean |
| **2. Finite, findable market** | 11,994 RCICs on a public register with names and locations | High — can reach every prospect |
| **3. Proven sales model** | Founder-led sales worked for Clio, Jobber, ServiceTitan, Jane App, Lawmatics | High — pattern holds across 10+ companies |
| **4. Validated by acquisition** | MyCase paid to acquire Docketwise for immigration vertical | High — PE firms see value |
| **5. Conversion proof** | Lawmatics' Sterling Immigration doubled conversions (9%→19%) | High — approach works |
| **6. Market timing** | 54% of RCICs are new (0-4 years), building practices from scratch | High — greenfield buyers |
| **7. Distribution channel exists** | CAPIC has 4,400 members in one forum + annual conference in your city | High — concentrated channel |

### 12.2 The 3 Things That Could Kill NeuronX (And How to Prevent Them)

| Risk | Kill Probability | Prevention |
|------|-----------------|------------|
| **Can't close first 5 clients** | Medium | Over-invest in demos. Do 50+ demos if needed. Offer founding pricing ($297/mo) for first 5 |
| **Clients churn after 2-3 months** | Medium | White-glove onboarding. Monthly check-ins. Show ROI dashboard (leads converted, time saved) |
| **Bigger player enters** | Low (12-18 months) | Move fast. 50 clients + CAPIC relationship + reviews = defensible within 12 months |

### 12.3 The "100 Firms" Execution Checklist

This is the single most important action:

```
Week 1:
□ Go to CICC public register (register.college-ic.ca)
□ Filter: Ontario → Active status → sort by firm
□ Find firms with 2+ consultants
□ Cross-reference: does the firm have a website? An inquiry form?
□ Build spreadsheet: Firm Name | RCIC Names | City | Website | Has Form? | LinkedIn URLs
□ Target: 100 firms in the list

Week 2-3:
□ Send 10 LinkedIn connection requests per day (personalized)
□ After accept: share one value insight (NOT a pitch)
□ After 2-3 value touches: offer 20-min demo
□ Book 3-5 demos per week

Week 4+:
□ Run demos. Close deals. Onboard personally.
□ After each onboarding: ask for Capterra review
□ After 30 days: ask for referral
□ Document everything → case study
```
