## 3. Pricing Strategy

### 3.1 Subscription Tiers

| Tier | Target Customer | Price | Included |
|------|-----------------|-------|----------|
| **Starter** | Solo agencies (1-20 clients) | $299/month | 100 playbook executions/month, 1 user, email support |
| **Growth** | Growing agencies (20-100 clients) | $799/month | 1,000 playbook executions/month, 5 users, priority support |
| **Enterprise** | Large agencies/SaaS platforms | Custom | Unlimited executions, white-label, dedicated support, SLA |

### 3.2 Pricing Philosophy

**Value-Based Pricing**:
- Agencies save 2-5 days per client deployment (worth $2K-5K in ops time)
- Our $299-799/month is 10-20% of the value we create
- Pricing anchored to alternative (hiring ops person = $5K+/month)

**Usage-Based Add-Ons**:
- Additional playbook executions: $0.50 per execution
- Additional users: $49/user/month
- Voice minutes: $0.05/minute (pass-through from providers)

### 3.3 Pricing Evolution

**Year 1**: Flat subscription (simplicity for early customers)  
**Year 2**: Usage-based components added (align cost with value)  
**Year 3**: Enterprise custom pricing (land-and-expand)
