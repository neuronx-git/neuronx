## CATEGORY NAME: Immigration Revenue Intelligence

Not a CRM. Not case management. The first Revenue Intelligence platform purpose-built for immigration consulting firms.

> NeuronX is the only platform that converts immigration inquiries into consultation-ready, scored, and briefed opportunities in under 5 minutes — without the RCIC lifting a finger until the consultation begins.

1. **Pre-Retainer Ownership** — Every competitor starts after the retainer. We own the 72 hours between "I'm interested" and "Here's my retainer."
2. **Five-Minute AI Response** — Average firm responds in 27 hours. We call in 5 minutes.
3. **Immigration-Native Intelligence** — Built by RCIC R705959 who processed 5,000+ cases. Not a generic CRM with immigration labels.

## BRANDING EVOLUTION

### Color System
| Color | Hex | Role |
|-------|-----|------|
| Deep Navy | #0A1628 | Primary backgrounds |
| Electric Indigo | #4F46E5 | Action color (buttons, links) |
| Coral | #E8380D | Urgency only (speed claims, alerts) |
| Emerald | #10B981 | Success states |
| Slate | #64748B | Secondary text |

### Typography
- **Headlines**: Satoshi (Fontshare, free)
- **Body**: General Sans (Fontshare, free)
- **Data/Metrics**: JetBrains Mono

### Visual Direction
- Dark-first (like Vercel, Linear)
- Gradient meshes for backgrounds
- Glassmorphism for product cards
- Dot grid textures for precision feel
- 12-16px rounded corners
